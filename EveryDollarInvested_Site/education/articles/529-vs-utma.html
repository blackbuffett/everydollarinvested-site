529 vs. UTMA

Every Dollar Invested Education • Updated 2025-08-29

529 plans are tax-advantaged for education; UTMAs are flexible for broader goals. Many families use both.

Feature529UTMA/UGMA
Primary useQualified educationAny purpose for the child
Tax benefitsTax-free growth for qualified expensesTaxed annually (kiddie tax rules)
ControlParent/owner retains controlChild assumes control at majority
